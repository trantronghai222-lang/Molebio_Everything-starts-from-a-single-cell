About Mole-Bio

Mole-Bio is a student-led molecular biology club for Lawrence S.Ting students in Ho Chi Minh City. We teach practical molecular biology techniques, share scientific knowledge, and empower local communities through science education and applied projects.

We believe in hands-on learning: every experiment teaches a lesson, every cell carries a story — everything starts from a single cell.

Activities & Teaching

  • Hands-on demonstrations: DNA extraction, PCR, gel electrophoresis, DNA models.
  • Workshops and club showcases for schools and community groups.
  • Student mentoring: help members run small research projects and write papers.

Community Projects

We are expanding into areas that help local rural communities tackle drought and flood problems by applying biosensing, water quality monitoring, and low-cost diagnostics.

Water Quality Kits

Low-cost field tests for contaminants using simple colorimetric assays.
